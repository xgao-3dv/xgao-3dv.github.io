Xiang Gao
xiang.gao@ia.ac.cn

I am an associate professor at Chinese Academy of Sciences, Institute of Automation (CASIA) since 2023.07. From 2019.08 to 2022.09, I spent three wonderful years at College of Engineering, Ocean University of China (OUC), as a postdoc and a lecturer. I received my Ph.D. degree from CASIA, where I was supervised by Prof. Zhanyi Hu and Prof. Shuhan Shen, and my M.S. and B.S. degrees both from OUC, where I was supervised by Prof. Zexiao Xie.

My research interest is 3D computer vision, with a particular interest in large-scale structure from motion and image-based 3D scene modeling.
